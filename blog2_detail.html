Play Forward By Marcus Rashford

Play Forward is the pioneering program from Marcus Rashford, Football Beyond Borders and Nike, encouraging the next generation to dream big on and off the pitch.

A co-curated curriculum delivered by trusted coaches helps young people from underserved communities develop valuable life and football skills.

"[Activism] goes hand in hand with what I'm doing on the pitch".

The Mission

Marcus Rashford, Nike, and Football Beyond Borders have teamed up to create "Play Forward," a program to raise the game for the next generation. The initiative starts in Greater Manchester and will scale to multiple cities in the UK.

Through Play Forward, we are leveraging the power of soccer to help 11–12-year-old girls and boys find their superpowers, build relationships, and dare to dream big.
