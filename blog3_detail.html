Trained Podcast: Find Authenticity With Fabian Domenech

By Nike • Yoga Teacher

Podcast Mindfulness Yoga

Discipline: Coaching

Nike yoga teacher Fabian Domenech has taught all over the world. Hear what he’s learned about himself and his practice along the way.

Over the past decade, yoga participation in the United States has nearly tripled. Fabian found his practice while juggling an aspiring fashion career, a service industry job, and CrossFit coaching in London.

Thanks to yoga, meditation, and extensive world travel, he’s learned the power of self-love and the importance of detachment.

In this episode, Fabian joins host Jaclyn Byrer to talk about mental health, community, belonging, and what students can expect from his classes.

"I think we need yoga in our lives somehow, just to be centered, to improve as humans..."

Fabian Domenech

Have a question about mindset, movement, nutrition, recovery or sleep? Or a suggestion for the podcast? Email Jaclyn at trained@nike.com.
